🎯 Breakeven Price & Exchange Rate Sensitivity

The USD price to sell your remaining shares without losing money. It can look high for three reasons: Spanish FIFO leaves your newest (often priciest) lots as the unsold ones; the cost basis is in euros and shown as a USD price at today's rate (a weaker dollar raises it); and the "full portfolio" line also has to recover any past realized losses.

💶 Dividends & interest income

In Spain, the dividends and interest you receive are taxed in the same "savings" pot as your stock gains. So money you already earned here this year pushes you up the tax brackets before you even sell a single share. Add these via menu option 3 ("Add Dividend/Interest Income").
